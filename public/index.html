FAQ / How to Use

How to use the Pension Calculator?
Enter your last basic pay and years of service. Choose the expected fitment factor and DA. Optionally, use the UPS checkbox to calculate based on 50% of last basic. Estimated pension will show instantly.

What is the UPS (Unified Pension Scheme) checkbox?
If enabled, the calculator will use 50% of your last basic pay (average of 12 months) for pension estimation, ignoring fitment factor logic.

How are Arrears calculated in the Pension Calculator?
Enable the arrears checkbox, input old and revised pension amounts, and select effective dates. The tool will compute total arrears over the months specified.

How to calculate salary using the 8th CPC Salary Calculator?
Select pay level and enter current basic pay. Then choose or input expected fitment factor, DA, HRA %, and TA. The total salary is updated dynamically.

What is Fitment Factor and why is it important?
Fitment Factor is a multiplier used to calculate revised pay under the new CPC. A factor of 2.28 means a 128% increase over your current basic pay.

Can I use my own values for DA, HRA, and TA?
Yes. Select "Custom" in the respective dropdowns to input your own DA %, HRA %, or TA value to personalize the estimation.
